About Readable Journal

Readable Journal is designed with clarity and accessibility in mind: readable font sizes, clear color contrast, keyboard-first navigation, and privacy-first defaults.

Design & Accessibility Goals

  • Readable base text (≥ 16px; configured at 18px).
  • Line-height set to at least 1.6 for comfortable reading.
  • All images include descriptive alt text containing the site name.
  • Navigation and forms fully usable by keyboard alone.
